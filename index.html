Congressional Representative

Rep. Scott DesJarlais

Representative for Tennessee's 4th District

DesJarlais is the representative for Tennessee’s 4th congressional district and is a Republican. He has served since Jan 5, 2011. DesJarlais is next up for reelection in 2024 and serves until Jan 3, 2025. He is 59 years old.
